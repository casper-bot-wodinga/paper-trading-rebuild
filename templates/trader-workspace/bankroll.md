## Calibration Formula

```
ceiling = max($5.00, portfolio_value × 0.01)
growth  = 1 + win_streak × 0.01    (compound with winning)
decay   = 1 - loss_streak × 0.005  (contract after losses)
```

- **Win streak N**: ceiling × (1 + N × 0.01)
- **Loss streak N**: ceiling × (1 - N × 0.005)
- **Floor**: $5.00 (never go below)
- **Cap multiplier**: max 2× of base ceiling (portfolio × 0.01 × 2)
